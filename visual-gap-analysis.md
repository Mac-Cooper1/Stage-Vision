- ✅ Olive tree added (signature element!)
- ✅ Jute rug (appropriate)
- ✅ Abstract art (right direction)
- ✅ Ceramic vase with olive branches (good)

**What's Missing:**

- ⚠️ No visible light rays through windows
- ⚠️ Shadows are flat, not dimensional
- ⚠️ Art is good but scale could be larger
- ⚠️ Overall color grade could be warmer
- ⚠️ Walls still feel slightly cool white

### Target Transformation (Adam Potts Standard)
Reference Images 2, 12 from portfolio showing:
- Dramatic golden light streaming through windows
- Rich warm shadows
- View through windows is soft/golden
- Furniture feels "placed" not "staged"
- Everything bathed in warm amber light

### Specific Prompt Improvement

```
CURRENT PROMPT LIKELY:
"Add dining table, wishbone chairs, olive tree, abstract art, jute rug"

IMPROVED PROMPT:
"LIGHTING FIRST: Transform the window light to golden hour quality - warm 
directional light streaming into the space creating rich, dimensional shadows 
on the floor and walls. Windows should glow with soft golden light.

STAGING: Replace furniture with solid light oak rectangular dining table 
with clean lines. Six Hans Wegner wishbone chairs in natural ash. Large 
jute rug with subtle texture extending well beyond chairs.

FOCAL WALL: Single large abstract painting (minimum 36x48 inches) in earth 
tones (terracotta, sage, cream, muted rust) with thin natural oak float frame.

SIGNATURE ELEMENTS: Tall olive tree (5-6 feet) in aged terracotta pot in 
corner near window. Table completely empty OR single large sculptural 
cream ceramic vase with 3-4 dried olive branches.

COLOR GRADING: Push entire image warmer - cream whites, golden tones, 
warm amber shadows. No cool tones. The light should feel like late 
afternoon sun."
```

## SUMMARY: THE THREE CRITICAL FIXES

### Fix #1: Lighting Must Be DRAMATIC
Current outputs are *warmer* but not *dramatic*. 

Add these specific lighting instructions to EVERY prompt:
- "visible light rays streaming through windows"
- "rich dimensional shadows"
- "golden hour quality throughout"
- "interior glow effect"

### Fix #2: Staging Must Be MORE SPECIFIC
Current outputs have right furniture types but feel generic.

Add these specifics:
- Exact furniture names (Hans Wegner wishbone, not just "wishbone chairs")
- Exact sizes ("36x48 inch painting" not just "large art")
- Exact placements ("angled near stove" not just "on counter")
- Exact quantities ("6 lemons" not just "citrus")

### Fix #3: Exteriors Need COMPLETE Transformation
Current exterior output is the weakest. It's barely transformed.

Exteriors MUST have:
- Golden hour sky (pink/orange/gold, not blue)
- Warm interior glow through ALL windows
- Trees catching golden light
- Overall "sunset magazine cover" feeling
